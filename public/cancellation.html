Cancellation Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Customer Cancellations

We understand that plans can change. Here is our cancellation policy:

  • More than 4 hours before the appointment: Free cancellation. No charges apply.
  • 2–4 hours before the appointment: A cancellation notice fee of ₹100 may apply to cover technician scheduling costs.
  • Less than 2 hours before the appointment: A visit charge of ₹150–₹200 may be applicable if the technician has already been dispatched.
  • No-show (customer not available): If the technician arrives and no adult is present to allow access, a visit charge may apply.

2. How to Cancel

To cancel a service appointment:

  • Call us at +91 92179 82869 with your booking details.
  • Email us at shinekitchensolutions@gmail.com with your booking details.
  • Reply to the confirmation SMS or call with a cancellation request.

3. Rescheduling

You may reschedule an appointment at any time by contacting us.

  • Rescheduling is subject to technician availability in your area.
  • There is no additional charge for rescheduling when done with adequate notice (more than 2 hours in advance).

4. Cancellation by Shine Kitchen Solutions

In rare circumstances, we may need to cancel or reschedule your appointment due to:

  • Technician unavailability due to emergency or illness
  • Unsafe weather or travel conditions
  • Technical reasons beyond our control

In such cases, we will notify you as soon as possible and offer a priority rebooking at no additional cost. No charges will apply for company-initiated cancellations.

5. Advance Payments

If you have paid an advance and cancel within the free cancellation window, your advance will be fully refunded within 5–7 business days. Cancellations outside the free window will be handled per the fee structure above.
