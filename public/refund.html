Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. General Refund Policy

We stand behind our services and your satisfaction with them is important to us. However, because our services are labor-based, we generally do not offer refunds once a service has been completed unless the issue was caused by our negligence.

2. Parts Guarantee

Any spare parts installed by our technicians come with their respective manufacturer's warranty. If a part fails within this period, it will be replaced at no extra cost for the part itself.
